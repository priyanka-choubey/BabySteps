How It Works

Treating skin has never been this easy

Step 1: Take a clear photo

As simple as that! Go to a well-lit room and snap a photo!

Step 2: We will assess your skin condition

In a matter of seconds, the Dermio app will send your issue to a dermatologist and provide you with a full diagnosis of your skin condition… just for $40.

Step 3: Your medicine is sent to your doorstep

You can have unlimited chat with the doctor and get a treatment plan. We will send the prescription to your nearest pharmacy.
